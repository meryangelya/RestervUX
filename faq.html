FAQ

What is Resterv?

Resterv is a website that provide an easy way to make reservation in your favorite restaurants. We have more than 12.310 restaurants as our tenants and 23.912 users. We provide a fast, efficient, and comfortable way to make reservation.

How do I make a reservation?

1. Click the Category menu, and choose the category

2. Click the restaurant to see the restaurant's information

3. If you have decided the restaurant, click the Book Now button on the restaurant's page

4. Fill in the Reservation Form and click Confirm.

Can I make a reservation for someone else?

Yes.

As long as the person that you made the reservation for know your informations that you use in Resterv.

How do I cancel my reservation?

To cancel a reservation, you can reach to our Customer Service for further assistance.
